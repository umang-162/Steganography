Hide one image in another!

This website demonstrates the principle of hiding information in an image. Upload the required image. The panel on the left should display the image you want to hide and the one on the right should display the image on which it needs to be hidden. After uploading both the images click on the encode button to display the resultant image. The resultant image will be displayed on the right panel. This method of hiding information in an image is known as Steganography.

After encoding an image you can download it and the use the decode feature below to unveil the information back again. Upload the modified image. After uploading it should be visible on the bottom left panel. Click on the decode button to extract the information. The hidden image should be visible in the right pane.
